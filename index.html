Meet The Founder

Meet Dr. Judith & Our Dedicated Team

Dr. Judith Founder & Lead Educator

At Dr. J Tutors, the future of your child's academics is in safe, professional, and passionate hands.

Founded by Dr. Ekemezie Okwuchi Judith, a medical doctor and passionate educator, the brand was built from a strong desire to make quality education more accessible, engaging, and result-driven for students across different academic levels.

We provide home tutoring and online tutoring for students across Nigeria, the UK, the US, Canada, and beyond, covering every curriculum and grade naming system. Whether your child is in Primary 1–6 · Grade 1–6 · Year 1–6 (primary level) or JSS1–SS3 · Grade 7–12 · Year 7–12 (secondary level), we have the right tutor for them. We support learners preparing for GCSE, IGCSE, WAEC, NECO, JAMB, SAT, A-Levels, and UK 11+ entrance exams. Our tutoring covers Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English Language, and other core subjects.

"We believe that no child is incapable of learning. Many students simply need the right teaching method, the right environment, and the right support system to thrive academically."

Clients love working with us because of our professionalism, intentional tutoring methods, flexible learning options, and our genuine commitment to student improvement. We empower young tutors by equipping them with the tools needed for effective online teaching, ensuring your child gets modern, top-tier instruction.
